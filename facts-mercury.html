    MERCURY
    ATMOSPHERE
    Mercury has an exosphere, an extremely thin and wispy atmosphere. It's so thin that it's often considered nearly absent compared to the dense atmospheres of other planets.
    GEOLOGY
    One of the most prominent features on Mercury is the Caloris Basin, a huge impact crater about 960 miles (1,550 kilometers) in diameter. It's surrounded by concentric rings and was formed by a massive impact early in Mercury's history.
    COMPOSITION
    Mercury's composition is predominantly rocky and metallic. It has a large metallic core, accounting for about 60% of its mass. This core is believed to be primarily composed of iron and is thought to be molten, generating a magnetic field.
