    VENUS
    ATMOSPHERE
    Despite being the second planet from the Sun, Venus rotates very slowly on its axis, taking around 243 Earth days to complete a single rotation. Interestingly, this means that a day on Venus (one full rotation) is longer than its year (orbit around the Sun), which takes about 225 Earth days.
    GEOLOGY
    Venus showcases unique geological formations called "coronae." These are circular to oval-shaped features formed by the upwelling of hot material from the interior, creating a raised ring with a central depression. These structures are believed to be related to mantle plumes or hotspots.
    COMPOSITION
    Venus' atmosphere contains clouds made of sulfuric acid droplets. These clouds cover the entire planet and contribute to the highly reflective nature of Venus, making it one of the brightest objects in the sky.
